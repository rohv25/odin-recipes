Sandwhiches are a classic food of all people all around the world. It's so simple to make! Its just two pieces of bread and toppings inside! This sandwhich will be a Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwhich.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Take two pieces of bread
  2. Put peanut butter on one side
  3. Put jelly on the other side
  4. Put it together
  5. ENJOY!
